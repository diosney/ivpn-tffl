Here’s How It Works

To register for Tax-Free For Life, you must be a United States resident, 18 years of age or older,
with a state driver’s license or government ID number.

  • Register Once.

    When you register for the official U.S. Government Lottery — available in all 50 states — you’ll be issued
    a unique "Tax-Free for Life" Player Number.

    This player number will serve as your permanent credential, providing secure access to all upcoming lottery entries and drawings.

  • What are your odds…. to Stop Paying Taxes.

    Your odds of winning each week are determined by the number of participants in the state where your driver's license or state ID was issued. They are not based on the total number of players nationwide.

    Every time you play, you’re contributing to a stronger Social Security system for all.

  • Drawing and Selection.

    A drawing is held in every state each Sunday and Thursday.

    One Tax-Free ID is picked in every state. If it’s yours, congratulations—you win!
